Burger

A burger is a classic comfort food, featuring a juicy patty nestled between soft buns. Topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauces, it’s a delicious handheld meal. Whether grilled, fried, or plant-based, burgers satisfy cravings with their rich flavors. Perfect with fries, they’re loved worldwide.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern delight, featuring marinated meat—chicken, beef, or lamb—slow-roasted on a vertical spit. Wrapped in warm pita with garlic sauce, pickles, and veggies, it’s a flavorful street food favorite. The smoky, juicy meat combined with fresh toppings makes shawarma an irresistible, satisfying meal.

Pizza

Pizza is a universally adored dish, featuring a crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and endless toppings. From pepperoni to veggies, every slice is a burst of flavor. Whether thin-crust or deep-dish, pizza is perfect for sharing, making it a go-to meal for any occasion.
